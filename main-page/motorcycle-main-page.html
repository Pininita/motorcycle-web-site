MT-09

The Yamaha MT-09 is a naked motorcycle with a powerful 847cc inline-three-cylinder engine that delivers an exciting blend of power and agility. With a modern and aggressive design, it is equipped with advanced technology such as adjustable riding modes and traction control. It stands out for its excellent handling in corners and its versatility both on the highway and in the city.
