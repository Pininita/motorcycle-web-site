CB650R

Here's where you discover the absolute magic of the four cylinders. The CB650R is light to deliver enjoyable performance from a city apple to a winding road. It boasts strong acceleration, nimble agility, and a refreshed Neo Sports Café style inspired by the CB1000R. Sometimes, less can truly be more because it adds superior technology and lets you have your first bike with Honda's new and impressive E-Clutch system.
